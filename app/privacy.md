# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 28, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the Jordan application ("the App") handles information when you use it.

## 1. Information We Collect

The App connects to a Jordan server that you configure. The following information may be processed:

- **Server connection data:** the server address and credentials you provide to connect to your Jordan server instance.
- **Task and message data:** status updates, messages, and task information exchanged between the App and the server you connect to.
- **Device information:** basic device identifiers used to register the client session with the server.

The App does not collect personal information beyond what is necessary to operate the connection to your configured server.

## 2. How We Use Information

Information collected by the App is used solely to:

- Establish and maintain the connection to your Jordan server.
- Display task statuses, messages, and actions from your server.
- Send commands and responses to your server on your behalf.

## 3. Data Storage

The App stores your server configuration (address, credentials) locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any third-party service. All task and message data resides on the Jordan server you configure and control.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to any third party. The only data exchange that occurs is between the App and the Jordan server you explicitly configure.

## 6. Security

Connection credentials are stored locally on your device. We recommend using secure, authenticated server configurations and keeping your device protected with a screen lock.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## 9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can open an issue in the project repository.
